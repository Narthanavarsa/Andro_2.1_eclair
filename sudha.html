"DC Icons Unmasked: The Faces Behind the Capes"

Step into the cinematic universe where justice wears a face, and legends walk among us. DC Icons Unmasked celebrates the brilliant actors who breathed life into the world’s most iconic superheroes and villains. From Henry Cavill’s stoic Superman to Margot Robbie’s wild Harley Quinn, these stars didn’t just wear capes — they carried legacies. Dive behind the scenes to uncover the charisma, craft, and transformation that turned comic book pages into unforgettable big-screen magic. Discover the real people behind the powers, and witness how Hollywood met heroism — one legendary performance at a time.
