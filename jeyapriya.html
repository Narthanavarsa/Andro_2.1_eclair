A novel indoline, pyrazole, and pyrrolizine integrated novel spirohetrocyclic hybrid, 1'-(3-(4-methoxyphenyl)-1-phenyl-1H-pyrazol-4-yl)-2-oxo-5',6',7',7a'-tetra hydrospiro[indoline-3,3'-pyrroli zine]-2',2'(1'H)-dicarbonitrile was synthesized from commercially available phenyl hydrazine by adopting condensation, cyclization followed by Vilsmeier-Haack reaction, Knoevenagel condensation and multi component one pot reaction strategies.

The target molecule was characterized by FT IR and 1H NMR spectroscopic methods and, the 13C NMR and mass spectral analysis is in progress.

Also, the synthesis of other derivatives of the target molecular hybrids is under progress.
